Products

  • Can you give me more information about P448® sneakers?

    P448® is an Italian streetwear sneaker label; a truly contemporary brand whose collections are designed to empower the individual.

    Our genderless sneakers are thoughtfully designed and crafted in Italy by master artisans for style pioneers across the globe. Defined by an eclectic aesthetic, we take inspiration from the streets, underpinned by the underground spirit of subculture. We create footwear with soul.

  • Are P448® sneakers Made in Italy?

    P448® products are all 100% made in Italy. Each sneaker is conceived and manufactured by master craftsmen in the finest factories in Italy, using rich materials, thoughtful details and best-in-class workmanship.
    the selection of rich materials, to the cutting of the leather, to the manual stitching of the sole, every step of the production process is executed with meticulous attention to detail.
    P448® design is engineered to achieve perfection in both form and function. An expert team of skilled artisans is devoted to quality and comfort in both design and performance.

  • Can you give me more information about a specific product?

    You can get in touch with the P448® staff by sending an email to info@p448.com to receive more information about a specific product. Our team is always available to help you.

  • My size is out of stock, when will it be available again?

    All P448® models are available on the official website p448.com. If the product you are looking for is not available, it may be out of stock or temporarily unavailable. We invite you to visit the site in the coming days as many models could be back online soon. If on the contrary we can help you choose a different model, please feel free to contact the P448® Staff by sending an email to info@p448.com.

  • Which stores carry P448® sneakers?

    There are many retailers where you can find your favorite P448® products. To receive a list of the retailers closest to you, please feel free to contact the P448® Staff by sending an email to info@p448.com.

  • I saw a pair of P448® sneakers in a store, but I’m not able to find them on the website. Why?

    If you saw a pair of P448® sneaker in a store but were not able to find them on the website, they are either an older model or out of stock.
